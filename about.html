Welcome! People call me Sush.

Since the last time I've updated this biography, I've gone from a freshman absolutely stoked to get into his dream school at UC Santa Barbara and make friends with every single student there, to a senior contemplating how to most memorably live out his last year with the friendships he's made and what kind of life he wants to live beyond college. I've gone through heartbreaks like I've never felt and victories I never dreamed of accomplishing. I've learned how catastrophic neglecting mental health can be and how achievable even the wildest aspirations are. I've found how there is always a way, but how sometimes it's better to just let it go.

There are some things that haven't changed, however. I still have a love for computer science and have a lifelong goal to create a positive impact on peoples' lives with my work.

Lately, I've been interested in machine learning & artificial intelligence, and have been working as an apprentice on the Question and Answering side of Amazon Alexa.

Anyways, I'm always down to grab a bite, grab a drink, surf a wave, snowboard the slopes, back to back DJ, 4 wheel a trail, play some Valorant, attend a music festival (OSL!), or try something new!
